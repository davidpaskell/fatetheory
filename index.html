Fate Theory

A Philosophical Framework

Fate Theory elevates the Biblical doctrine of God's providence to a Fated state that still allows man to choose through divine conditional operations.

This theory is currently more of a foundational framework than it is something like a complete system of thought or a theory of everything. However, it is still comprehensive and can be expanded on indefinitely (which I have some ideas on how to do that I am currently working on). It is currently found in its latest form in my book, linked below. This book is 170 pages in this ebook pdf format but reads more like 85 pages. It will remain free as an ebook always, but I will consider publishing hard copies available to buy if in demand: Two to Sin One to Win
