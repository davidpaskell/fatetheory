Fate Theory

A Philosophical Framework

Fate Theory equates the metaphysical concept of Reality as we know it to the Biblical character of God. This equation then elevates the Biblical doctrine of God's providence to a Fated state, which means that Reality = God = Fate. In this state, the development of nature and living beings is theorized to be inevitable, including the forming of people who have the minds capable of choice. In this state, God, must then do the greatest good to maintain balance to the universe, which includes acting on man's choice based on what they give Him permission to do.

This theory is currently more of a framework to be expanded on than it is something like a complete system of thought or a theory of everything, but it is similar in the sense that it can be expanded on indefinitely. So, I am not too worried about copyright issues; it is more of an opensource project than a book to sell. The theory integrates well with Chris Langan's Cognitive Theoretical Model of the Universe as well as existing extradimensional theories of the universe, like that of an inflationary multiverse, a quantum realm, the mathematical universe hypothesis or string theory.

This foundational framework is explained more thoroughly in my book, linked below. This book is 170 pages in this ebook pdf format but reads more like an 85 page book. The book is free unless people demand hard copies (in which case I will take the money): Two to Sin One to Win
